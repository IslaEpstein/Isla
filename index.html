About Me

Hi, I’m Isla! I’m 13 years old (I just turned 13 on September 6!) and I’m in seventh grade. I have a younger sister and a mini golden doodle named Milo. My two biggest passions are alpine ski racing ⛷️ and soccer ⚽.

Skiing

In the winter, I ski at Waterville Valley and go to school at Waterville Valley Academy. I am a second-year U14 ski racer. Over Thanksgiving, I travel to Colorado to ski, and during the summer I also train on snow at Mt. Hood.

Soccer

I play soccer for both my school and my club team. I love competing, working hard, and being part of a team with my friends.

Travel

I love traveling with my family. Every summer we go on a trip to a new place. So far, I’ve been to Greece, England, and Mexico. Next year, we’re planning to visit Africa, and I’m super excited!

Family & Traditions

I love holidays and family traditions. On Christmas, I visit my grandparents on my mom’s side and see my family. Before Christmas, I always host a cookie decorating party. On Easter, my mom and dad hide eggs for me and my sister, and then we open our baskets together. I also babysit my baby cousins, which I enjoy a lot.
