Hi, I'm Isla!

About Me

I’m 13 years old (my birthday is September 6 🎉) and I’m in seventh grade. I love alpine ski racing and soccer, and I’m always working hard to improve. I’m also a second-year U14 skier, which keeps me busy with training and races.

Skiing

In the winter, I ski at Waterville Valley and attend Waterville Valley Academy. Over Thanksgiving I travel to Colorado to ski, and in the summer I train at Mt. Hood. Skiing is a huge part of my life, and I love the feeling of flying down the mountain.

Soccer

I play soccer for both school and club. I enjoy being part of a team, competing in games, and always working to get faster and stronger.

Family & Traditions

I live with my younger sister, and we always have fun together. I also have a mini golden doodle named Milo 🐶, who is always making me smile.

Family traditions are really important to me. Every Christmas, we go to my grandparents’ house on my mom’s side, and I get to spend time with my cousins (I usually babysit the younger ones). Before Christmas, I always host a cookie-decorating party. On Easter, my parents hide eggs for me and my sister to find before we open our baskets.

Adventures

In the summer, I go to sleepaway camp, love visiting the beach, and travel with my family. We’ve been to Greece, England, and Mexico, and next year we’re going to Africa! Each summer we pick a new place to explore.
